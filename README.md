# CGPA Calculator (AI & DS - 2022 Regulation)

A simple, effective, and web-based CGPA Calculator built specifically for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI & DS) students under the 2022 regulation. This tool allows users to easily input their grades and accurately track their academic performance across multiple semesters.

**Live Demo:** [Access the Calculator Here](https://sri-kanth-j.github.io/gpa)

## Features

* **Curriculum Auto-Loading:** Automatically loads the specific subjects for the AI & DS curriculum (2022 Regulation).
* **Semester Tracking:** Supports calculations for Semesters 1 through 6. *(Note: Selecting more than 6 semesters will automatically trigger a page reload as a built-in constraint).*
* **Qualitative Grading:** Automatically interprets your numerical GPA into a qualitative grade (e.g., Excellent, Good, Average).
* **High Accuracy:** Calculation logic has been tested against original institutional mark sheets for high accuracy.

## Technologies Used

* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
* **Hosting:** GitHub Pages

## How to Use

Follow these steps to calculate your GPA:

1. **Open the Calculator:** Navigate to `sri-kanth-j.github.io/gpa` on your device.
2. **Select Semesters:** Choose the number of semesters you wish to include in your calculation from the input field (Maximum: 6). 
3. **Input Grades:** The relevant AI & DS subjects will load automatically. Select the grade you received (e.g., A, B+, C) for each subject.
4. **Calculate:** Click the **Calculate** button at the bottom of the form.
5. **Review Results:** The calculator will output your numerical CGPA (the average of grade points earned across all subjects) and your qualitative grade.

## Qualitative Grade Interpretation

Based on the calculated GPA, the system provides the following qualitative feedback:

* **Excellent:** 9.0 and above
* **Very Good:** 8.0 – 8.9
* **Good:** 7.0 – 7.9
* **Average:** 6.0 – 6.9
* **Below Average / Fail:** Below 6.0 (as per institution rules)

## Limitations & Notes

* **Curriculum Specific:** The subject data is strictly limited to the 2022 regulation for AI & DS of my collage.
* **Semester Cap:** Data is currently mapped for Semesters 1 through 6 only.
